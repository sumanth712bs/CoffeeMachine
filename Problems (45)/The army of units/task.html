The army of units Open on JetBrains Academy

In a computer game, each gamer has an army of units.

Write a program that classifies the army of your enemies in accordance with these rules:

Units:  Category
less than 1: no army
from 1 to 4: few
from 5 to 9: several
from 10 to 19: pack
from 20 to 49: lots
from 50 to 99: horde
from 100 to 249: throng
from 250 to 499: swarm
from 500 to 999: zounds
1000 and more: legion

The program is to read the number of units and to output the corresponding category.


Sample Input:
5
Sample Output:
several

Sample Input:
303
Sample Output:
swarm


Memory limit: 256 Mb
Time limit: 5s
